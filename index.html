About

Follow Me:

Born and raised in the most luxurious city Dubai, gives me an edge to easily work with a diverse set of clients. When a client comes to me, I want them to have a focused, conscious and subconscious experience as a result of our time together. My sessions are compassionate, intuitive, and reflect a jovial and spiritual nature. They are deep, yet light hearted, and offer an opportunity for deep self-awareness, and transformation

I want you to know that your pain and lives have meaning. I want you to make sense of your issues and to heal from it in return. I strongly believe that every experience that you have gone through has meaning and also an associated metaphysical reasoning. This pain can be traced back to your current life (pre-natal stage, birth, childhood), your past-lives or could be a borrowed issue that has gripped you over time

For me, it all started with an intense first-hand experience by regressing into a past life. My own personal journey then led me to research, study further and make it my full-time career. I have been practising Past-Life Regression Therapy since 2017 and later went on to be trained as a Clinical Hypnotherapist. I’m also a certified Transactional Analyst, QHHT Practitioner (Quantum Healing Hypnosis Technique trained by Julia Cannon), TASSO practitioner (Post-Graduation in Transpersonal Regression Therapy) and lots more. I’m of the belief that learning and unlearning is only one aspect of being a practitioner. It also requires focus, commitment, continuous reading, conversations, meditation, own experiential healing, self-trust, practise and being attuned to cosmic energies around you
